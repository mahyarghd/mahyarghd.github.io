About Me

I am Mahyar Gholizadeh, an enthusiastic researcher and engineer specializing in **Image Processing, Machine Learning, and Artificial Intelligence**. With a strong academic background in **Electrical and Computer Engineering**, I have dedicated my studies and professional journey to advancing computer vision and deep learning techniques for medical applications.

I hold a **Master’s degree** from *Babol Noshirvani University of Technology*, where I worked on applying machine learning algorithms to medical imaging, specifically in **MRI-based diagnosis of muscle rupture**. My research has provided me with hands-on experience in **Python, MATLAB, OpenCV, TensorFlow, and PyTorch**.

Beyond academics, I am an active member of **the Iranian Society of Machine Vision and Image Processing (ISMVIP)**, where I have led workshops and technical discussions on AI-driven image analysis. I am also passionate about **IoT, embedded systems, and data science**, constantly exploring new technologies that merge hardware and AI.

Currently, I am looking forward to furthering my expertise by pursuing a Master’s program at a prestigious university, where I can contribute to cutting-edge research in **computer vision and biomedical engineering**.
